JPL on the Go for Mobile

Access the latest news, videos and images from your phone with our specially formatted, easy-to-browse mobile sites. Find out how we're bringing our space to yours, and take a look at some of the exciting features you'll find with "JPL on the Go!"

Smartphone Mobile Experience

The next time you type www.jpl.nasa.gov into your smartphone's browser -- whether you're using an iPhone, BlackBerry or any other smartphone -- you'll automatically be redirected to our newly optimized mobile site. You'll find your favorite features from our standard website (news, videos and images), but in a format that's easy to browse on the go.

Features

  • Read top stories and news features in a smartphone-friendly format
  • Watch JPL videos and animations
  • Get detailed summaries of past, present and future missions
  • Browse by topic, and discover what's happening across the universe
  • Bookmark us for one-touch access from your smartphone's home screen
  • Access JPL's educational activities and resources
  • Images, multimedia and interactives

    Get even more features on the go with JPL Apps for iPhone and iPod Touch devices. And find out how JPL's newest "Space Images" app is bringing the universe to your fingertips

Not A Smartphone User?

Type in the same URL (www.jpl.nasa.gov) from your mobile device, and you'll automatically be connected to our text-based site. Streamlined content means fast download speeds for your news stories and features, plus plenty of screen space to spare.
